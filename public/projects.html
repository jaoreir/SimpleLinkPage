Projects

Here are some projects I've worked on.

Motion Control Fighting Game - Solar Fairies

A project that I worked on is a motion controlled 1v1 fighting game. To play, you have to strike certain poses with your body to make your fairy character attack, defend, and evade. For this project, we used the Azure Kinect DK camera to do body tracking. I also did the sound design for this game.

Post-Stroke Rehabilitation Program - Hand Tracking Minigames

Another project I've had the pleasure of working on had us developing minigames for post-stroke patients. The games are simple, in which the patient has to act out certain tasks using hand motions that benefit their post-stroke rehabilitation e.g. wrist & elbow rotations. The movement of their hands is tracked using specialized cameras, and recorded for later analyzing and comparison.

Spraypaint Interactive Installation

I've also programmed a spray paint system where you can do digital graffiti. The system was used in 2021 for Kaohsiung City's Christmas Exhibition.
